Chakrasana

About Chakrasana (Wheel Pose)

Chakrasana, also known as Wheel Pose, is a powerful backbend that improves flexibility and strengthens the entire body. It opens up the chest and lungs, making breathing easier and more effective.

Benefits

  • Improves spinal flexibility
  • Strengthens arms, legs, and core
  • Opens chest and improves breathing
  • Boosts energy and reduces stress

Steps to Perform

  • Lie flat on your back
  • Bend your knees and place feet on the floor
  • Place hands near shoulders
  • Lift your body into an arch shape
  • Hold for a few seconds and breathe
  • Slowly come back down
