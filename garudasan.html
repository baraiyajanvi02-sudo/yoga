Garudasana

About Garudasana (Eagle Pose)

Garudasana, also known as Eagle Pose, is a balancing yoga posture that improves focus, coordination, and flexibility. It stretches the shoulders, arms, and legs while enhancing stability.

Benefits

  • Improves balance and concentration
  • Strengthens legs and ankles
  • Stretches shoulders and upper back
  • Enhances body coordination

Steps to Perform

  • Stand straight with feet together
  • Bend slightly and balance on one leg
  • Wrap the other leg around it
  • Cross your arms and bring palms together
  • Hold position and breathe slowly
  • Release and repeat on other side
