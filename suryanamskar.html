Suryanamaskar (Sun Salutation)

Suryanamaskar is a sequence of twelve powerful yoga poses. It energizes the body, strengthens muscles, improves flexibility, and stimulates internal organs.
Benefits: - Improves posture
- Boosts metabolism
- Increases flexibility
- Reduces stress and anxiety
Steps: 1. Start in Mountain Pose
2. Stretch arms upward
3. Forward bend
4. Step back into plank
5. Cobra Pose
6. Downward Dog
7. Step forward
8. Rise back to Mountain Pose
